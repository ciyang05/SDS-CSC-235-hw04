About the Matrix

This matrix shows genre to genre relationships. The darker blues indicate that the two genres are frequently cited together, while a lighter blue indicates that the genres are less frequently identified together in under one movie. A white cell indicates that there is no data for the specific genre combination. The rows and columns detail each genre.

About the Force Graph

This visualization shows the co-occurance between actors in Netflix movies. Each node represents an actor, and a link between these actors represents a movie or movies they were both casted in. Due to the nature of the dataset, only 30 of the most connected actors are shown. This means the visualization only depends on a subset of the entire dataset.

User Interactions:

  • Hover over a node to see the specific actor and number of connections they have
  • Drag a node to highlight specific connections
